Our mission

Our mission is to eliminate food waste and combat hunger by seamlessly connecting generous food donors with individuals and organizations in need. We aim to build a sustainable and supportive community where surplus food is redistributed to those who need it most, ensuring everyone has access to nutritious meals. By fostering a culture of sharing and responsibility, we strive to reduce the environmental impact of food waste while addressing food insecurity. Together, we can create a more equitable and sustainable world.

Who are we?

We are a dedicated community committed to reducing food waste and fighting hunger. Our platform connects generous food donors with individuals and organizations in need, ensuring surplus food is redistributed rather than discarded. By fostering a culture of sharing and sustainability, we aim to provide nutritious meals to those who need them most while minimizing our environmental footprint. Together, we strive to create a more equitable and hunger-free world, one meal at a time.

How does this work?

At our core, we facilitate the seamless connection between generous food donors and individuals or organizations in need. Donors begin by creating adverts detailing surplus food available for donation, specifying quantities, types, and any delivery preferences. These adverts are then published on our platform and displayed on an interactive map, allowing those in need to easily locate nearby donations. Once a suitable donation is found, donors and recipients connect to coordinate collection or delivery, ensuring that surplus food reaches those who need it most. Through this process, we not only reduce food waste but also fight hunger and promote a more sustainable and compassionate community.
Join us in our mission to make a difference, one meal at a time.
